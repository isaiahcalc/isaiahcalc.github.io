Introduction

The Isaiah is a unit of measurement which is used to measure the cost effectiveness of alcohol (i.e the price:pissed ratio). What 1i actually represents is 1g of pure alcohol per unit of currency. Although this value by itself isn't particularly useful, when the cost-effectiveness of multiple drinks is calculated using this app, whichever has a higher Isaiah value will result in a greater level of intoxication for the price.
